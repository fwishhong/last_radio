## Long Description

### 中文

《末日电台：旧体育馆守夜》是一款**单人**的章节小品：你是旧体育馆避难点的值班员，要在连续 10 个夜晚里守住这座建筑。

**白天——做决策**
在有限的木板、零件、电池和药品之间权衡：加固门窗？搜救更多避难者？还是开广播、扩大信号覆盖？每个选择都会改变下一夜你能调动的资源。

**夜晚——你亲自上场**
第一夜起，唯一的在场角色就是你自己。门被撞、窗被砸、发电机掉电、避难者恐慌——你需要在它们之间奔走，**同一个晚上同时守多处**。Nora 和 Elias 会陆续加入，但他们不是无敌的伙伴，也要休息、也会受挫。

**电台——决定生死的拨盘**
每晚电台会响起呼叫。Victor 的频道稳定，Elias 的频道靠近新天线时才会清晰，干扰频道拨到算接错。三秒接通 +1 信任，错 +0.5 暴露度——这是你联络外部世界的少数几条线。

**失败不是终点**
失守一夜会有报告：坚持了多久、修复了哪些门、失守了几次、资源还剩多少。报告可以读档重打，也可以第二天从头开始。

- 中英双语，所有 UI / 资源 / 剧情 / 报告文案
- 3 个存档槽 + 难度选择 + 通关 N+
- 程序化音乐 + AI 生成的 BGM / 美术
- 完整 Steam 集成（云存档 / 成就 / Rich Presence）
- 无内购、无抽卡、无体力

### English

**Last Radio: Old Stadium Watch** is a single-player chapter-sized game. You play as the night-shift warden of an old stadium turned shelter, and you must hold it for ten consecutive nights.

**By day, choose carefully.**
Planks, parts, batteries, and medicine are limited. Reinforce the doors? Search for survivors? Or boost the broadcast to widen the radio's reach? Every choice reshapes what you can pull off the next night.

**By night, you are the one on the floor.**
From night one, the only body physically present in the stadium is you. The front door buckles. The left window rattles. The generator drops. Survivors panic. You run between them all, often juggling two or three crises at once. Nora and Elias will join you over time — they help, but they tire, and they make mistakes.

**The radio is a literal dial.**
Each night the radio calls. Victor's channel is steady; Elias's channel only goes clear when the new antenna is up; the static channel is a deliberate trap — tune there and you take exposure. Three seconds of correct tuning earns one contact and +1 trust; wrong tuning costs +0.5 exposure. The radio is one of your few lifelines to the world outside.

**Failure is not the end.**
A lost night gets a report: how long you held, which doors you repaired, how many breaches, what's left in the stores. You can retry from the report or start the next day fresh.

**Features**
- Full 10-night chapter-one campaign
- Solo indie short, ~3-5 hours to clear
- Bilingual: every UI string, art caption, story beat, and report line
- 3 save slots + difficulty choice + NG+ after clearing
- Procedural music layered with AI-generated BGM and ambience
- Full Steam integration: cloud saves, achievements, Rich Presence
- No microtransactions, no gacha, no energy timers
