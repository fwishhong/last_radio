# NightShiftGame Audio Prompts

Use these as Suno prompts. Prefer instrumental exports with no vocals and no lyrics. Keep the tracks loopable, restrained, and built around old-stadium space, generator hum, analog radio noise, low strings, muted percussion, and sparse piano. Avoid EDM drops, big trailer drums, heroic brass, choir, and bright pop structure.

Export or rename the files into `assets/final/night_shift/audio/`. The game accepts `.ogg`, `.mp3`, and `.wav`.

## Required Music

### `music_cover`

Instrumental game title theme for a survival story in an abandoned indoor stadium shelter. Analog radio static, lonely piano notes, warm but haunted low strings, subtle tape hiss, distant generator hum. Slow, cinematic ambient, 70 BPM, bittersweet but tense, no vocals, no lyrics, seamless loop, about 90 seconds.

### `music_day`

Instrumental daytime planning ambience in an old gymnasium shelter. Dusty table, lantern hum, quiet people sleeping nearby, soft felt piano, muted strings, gentle analog noise, restrained hope, 65 BPM, no vocals, no lyrics, loopable, about 90 seconds.

### `music_night`

Instrumental survival tension loop for nights inside an old stadium. Distant door impacts, generator hum, analog radio static pulses, low synth drone, sparse metallic percussion, anxious but not action-trailer style, 80 BPM, no vocals, no lyrics, seamless loop, about 90 seconds.

### `music_night_late`

Instrumental late-night crisis loop for a besieged stadium shelter. Failing power, antenna static, low bowed metal, heartbeat-like muted percussion, cold air through windows, rising dread, 86 BPM, no vocals, no lyrics, seamless loop, about 90 seconds.

### `music_night_final`

Instrumental final-stand survival music in an old stadium under siege. Urgent generator pulse, radio distress beacons, restrained percussion, low strings, emotional pressure, no heroic brass, no choir, no vocals, no lyrics, 92 BPM, seamless loop, about 2 minutes.

### `music_final`

Instrumental dawn ending theme after ten nights in an old stadium shelter. Survivors reach sunrise; one distant radio operator sacrificed himself to keep the channel alive. Fragile piano, soft strings, fading radio static, warm sunrise feeling, bittersweet and moving but restrained, no vocals, no lyrics, 70 BPM, about 2 minutes.

## Optional Report Tracks

### `music_success`

Short instrumental successful night report cue. Exhausted relief after surviving until dawn, soft piano chord, quiet radio confirmation, gentle strings, no vocals, no lyrics, understated, 30 to 45 seconds.

### `music_failure`

Short instrumental failed night report cue. Breached shelter at night, low piano hit, radio dropout, cold wind, subdued grief, no gore, no vocals, no lyrics, restrained, 30 to 45 seconds.

### `music_report`

Neutral instrumental report-screen ambience. Clipboard, old radio desk, low room tone, soft tape hiss, sparse piano notes, tired but focused, no vocals, no lyrics, loopable, about 60 seconds.
